# Deck Symbolism Engine

## Purpose

The symbolism engine keeps cards meaningful while the Deck DNA engine keeps them visually cohesive.

## Symbol layers

- Tarot archetypes and traditional meanings.
- Oracle themes such as renewal, courage, sovereignty, shadow, and intuition.
- Numerology associations for numbers, life paths, and cycles.
- Philosophy prompts from Stoic, Socratic, Hermetic, Neoplatonic, and contemplative traditions.
- Recurring deck motifs such as moons, keys, birds, gardens, temples, scrolls, thresholds, and celestial maps.

## Consistency rules

Each card can inherit recurring motifs while expressing a distinct archetype. The engine should avoid repeating identical compositions across a full deck.
